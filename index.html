About

My name is Alex Lopez, I am a second year at Drexel University majoring in Computing and Security Technologies. I have always had an interest in technology dating back to the summer of seventh grade where I built a laptop using a Raspberry Pi at a computer camp. I kept that passion for technology going into highschool where in my sophomore year I build a pc from scratch.

I first became interested in cyber security when I read the book Countdown to Zero Day: Stuxnet and the Launch of the World's First Digital Weapon by Kim Zetter. I became enthralled in cybersecurity and have remained so until this day. I love that cybersecurity is an always evolving field, there is always more to learn.
