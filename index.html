About

My name is Alex Lopez. I am a third year at Drexel University majoring in Computing and Security Technologies. I have always had an interest in technology dating back to the summer of seventh grade where I built a laptop using a Raspberry Pi at a computer camp. I kept that passion for technology going into high school, where in my sophomore year I assembled a PC from scratch.

I first became interested in cyber security when I read the book Countdown to Zero Day: Stuxnet and the Launch of the World's First Digital Weapon by Kim Zetter. I love that cybersecurity is an evolving field; there is always more to learn.
